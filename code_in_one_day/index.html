ABOUT ME

Hello! I am a UX designer from Los Angeles with a background in social media, digital marketing and graphic design.

I’m fascinated with finding creative ways to solve problems and tell stories, even the messy process it entails. I thrive in situations that blend creativity with logic and reason, from designing something beautiful to analyzing problems to their core.

I’m passionate about volunteering my skills for important causes, particularly political activism, gun control and helping those less fortunate. Currently, I’m a designer and committee member for nonprofit Sugar & Stilettos, a small but mighty all-female team that’s fundraised over $200k for the Westside Food Bank.
